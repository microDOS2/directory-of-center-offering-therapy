Regulatory Information

Colorado

Proposition 122 (Natural Medicine Health Act) passed November 2022. Applications opened December 31, 2024.

  • Age 21+ required
  • No prescription needed
  • Standard & Micro healing center licenses
  • DMT, Ibogaine, Mescaline possible after 2026
Natural Medicine Division DORA

Oregon

Measure 109 passed November 2020. First licensed service center opened June 2023 (EPIC Healing Eugene).

  • First state to legalize (2020)
  • Only Psilocybe cubensis permitted
  • No take-home products
  • Out-of-state clients welcome
Oregon Health Authority
